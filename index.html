The Robotics and
Embedded Systems Club
EMBEDDO aims to foster a culture of technological innovation and research among students at the Faculty of Technology, University of Delhi. Our mission is to provide a platform for students to learn, innovate and excel in the fields of innovative robotics, automation and other related technologies.

Our Objectives

  1. 1 Educate members on fundamentals as well as advanced concepts of robotics and automation.
  2. 2 Not only encourage innovative thinking but also inculcate problem-solving through hands-on project.
  3. 3 Prepare and support members participating in various national and international competitions.
  4. 4 Promote research and facilitate the publication of findings in reputable journals.
  5. 5 Mentor junior members to ensure the continuity and growth of the club.
